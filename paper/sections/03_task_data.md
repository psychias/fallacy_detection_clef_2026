# 3. Task and Data

## 3.1 The three subtasks

The Touché 2026 lab [Heinrich et al. 2026] (task description and labels at https://touche.webis.de/clef26/touche26-web/fallacy-detection.html) defines three subtasks over the Reddit informal-fallacy data of Sahai et al. [2021], with evaluation hosted on TIRA [Fröbe et al. 2023]. ST1 is a binary decision on whether a given comment, presented together with its parent comment and the title of the originating thread, commits any informal fallacy. ST2 conditions on a positive ST1 decision and asks for the specific fallacy type from an eight-way inventory: authority, black_white, hasty_generalization, natural, population, slippery_slope, tradition, worse_problems. ST3 conditions on a negative ST1 decision and asks for the argument scheme of the comment from a four-way inventory operationalised by the lab task description [Heinrich et al. 2026], whose conceptual antecedents are the Walton-Reed-Macagno scheme catalogue [Walton, Reed, Macagno 2008] and the argumentation-profile framing of Macagno [2022]: pi (practical-internal), pe (practical-external), ei (epistemic-internal), ee (epistemic-external). The pi/pe/ei/ee abbreviations are the labels used throughout our internal logs and the rest of this notebook. The label set positions naturally inside the broader fallacy taxonomies surveyed by Helwe et al. [2024], although we do not retrain or evaluate on MAFALDA. Evaluation is by macro-averaged F1 in all three subtasks, which puts roughly equal weight on each label and turns rare-class behaviour into a binding constraint on the headline number — a point we return to in Section 6.3.

## 3.2 Base and enhanced tracks

Each subtask is evaluated in two tracks. The base track exposes only original Reddit fields — in our setup, text_raw, text_raw_parent, text_raw_title, text_base, argument_base — which the model concatenates into a single input. The enhanced track additionally exposes two organiser-supplied paraphrases, text_enhanced and argument_enhanced, that were rewritten with knowledge of the gold labels in order to make the argumentative structure of the comment more explicit. We flag the rewriting-with-label-knowledge here because it directly affects how the enhanced-track numbers should be read; we defer the quantitative diagnostic and the defensive framing to Section 6.2, where the four-part argument can be made in one place. The lab permits up to three runs per (subtask, track), for a maximum of nine submissions; we used six (one per cell), populating one slot per subtask per track and not using the additional two-runs-per-track allowance.

## 3.3 Dataset statistics

Following the official Touché 2026 release, we hold out a stratified development partition from the published training data; the ST3 partition contains 473 examples. For ST1 and ST2 we adopt the analogous stratified split; exact sizes are deferred to the camera-ready once the test phase concludes. The ST3 partition is the one we use for the 5-fold cross-validation runs reported in Section 5 (exp_0124 defines the stratified fold assignments and exp_0153 runs them under our submitted ST3-enhanced recipe). Class distributions follow the underlying Sahai et al. data and are long-tailed; the ST3 pe class in particular is small enough to be the binding constraint on macro-F1, which we revisit in Section 4.7 and Section 6.3.
